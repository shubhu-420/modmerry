About Us

Your beauty, Our passion

Getting married in Varanasi and having trouble finding a bridal makeup artists? Well Mod Merry Salon is one of the best wedding makeup artist based out of Varanasi. Mod Merry Salon is professional bridal makeup artist, specializing in bridal makeup and can provides services to doll you up for your wedding and pre wedding functions. The team at Mod Merry Salon is an expert when it comes to wedding day makeup, and has received excellent reviews by all the brides we have worked on till date. We work closely with all brides, personalizing the entire process and instilling confidence on the big day. The team at Mod Merry Salon loves to play with makeup, hairstyling and making people look their best during the wedding festivities
